ABOUT me

I'm a recent Data Science graduate from Arizona State University with a deep love for analytics, math, and all things AI. There's something incredibly satisfying about taking messy, chaotic data and transforming it into meaningful insights that actually make a difference. Whether I'm building predictive models, exploring machine learning algorithms, or just nerding out over a beautifully clean dataset, I'm in my element. I thrive at the intersection of logic and creativity — because data isn't just numbers, it's storytelling.

Outside of the data world, I'm a bit of a creative chameleon. I love design, content creation, and finding new ways to make things look and feel intentional. You'll probably find me lifting weights at the gym, attempting to become a runner (emphasis on attempting), or at home surrounded by my cats and a growing jungle of houseplants. I'm also lowkey a gamer when time allows. I believe life is about balance — between the analytical and the artistic, the serious and the silly, the grind and the things that make you feel alive.
